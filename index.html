Beautiful Treasure: The Confident Me Program Overview

This comprehensive 6-week Social-Emotional Learning curriculum is designed to help elementary girls (Ages 6-10) develop a strong sense of self-worth, build emotional resilience, and master the social skills needed to navigate their world with kindness and confidence.

Week 1: Me, Myself, and I

Core Focus: Discovering Identity

  • My Unique Gem: List three things that make you special and unlike anyone else!
  • Favorite Things Quiz: Choose your favorite color, food, and animal to create your personal profile!

Week 2: My Superpower: Confidence

Core Focus: Positive Self-Talk

  • Affirmation Station: Write one confident sentence about yourself to save in your confidence jar!
  • Confidence Walk: Practice your superpower pose in the mirror!

Week 3: Dealing with Big Feelings & Bullies

Core Focus: Resilience & Safety

  • Feelings Check-in: How do you feel today? Identify the emotion and pick a calm-down tool.
  • The Strong Voice: Write down three ways you can tell a bully 'STOP' clearly and firmly.

Week 4: Being a Great Friend

Core Focus: Empathy and Kindness

  • Kindness Ripple Log: What is one kind thing you did for someone else this week?
  • Empathy Glasses: Imagine your friend is sad. What do you think they need right now?

Week 5: Making and Keeping Friends

Core Focus: Social Skills

  • Conversation Starter Drill: Practice two ways to say hello to a new person (e.g., 'I like your...').
  • Friendship Recipe: List the 3 best 'ingredients' for a good friendship (e.g., listening, sharing).

Week 6: Powerful Communication

Core Focus: Assertive Voice

  • The 'I Feel' Builder: Write one 'I feel...' statement about something that bothered you today.
  • Active Listening Game: List the three rules of being a super listener (look, wait, don't interrupt).

This program directly addresses common challenges faced by girls today, providing practical tools for confidence, conflict resolution, and positive peer engagement.
